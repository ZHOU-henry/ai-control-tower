# Six-Track Portfolio Expansion

## Decision

The portfolio should expand from three long-horizon tracks to six:

1. `Agora`
2. `Physis`
3. `Titan`
4. `Noesis`
5. `Peras`
6. `Stoicheia`

## Why

Henry's long-term ambition now clearly spans three additional domains that are not reducible to the original three tracks:

- artificial consciousness and related governance / observability questions
- computability limits and durable human value
- the six-factor structure of the AI and digital economy

Keeping these as explicit tracks is better than smuggling them into the original repos, where they would distort scope and blur ownership.

## Rule

These new tracks are official portfolio members, but they should still begin with narrow, defensible MVP wedges.

Large ambition is allowed.

Scope inflation without executable boundaries is not.
