# Portfolio Overview

## System

Henry operates a four-agent loop through OpenClaw and Feishu:

- `Athena`: project control
- `Hermes`: research
- `Hephaestus`: development
- `Themis`: audit

## Product Tracks

- `Agora` - AI Agent Platform
  First shipping MVP. AI Agent marketplace and operator workspace.
- `Physis` - Physical AI Framework
  Simulator-first Physical AI framework.
- `Titan` - Embodied Intelligence Research
  Long-horizon embodied intelligence research track with strict safety framing.
- `Noesis` - Artificial Consciousness Observatory
  Long-horizon track for monitoring, modeling, governance, and advancement under the possibility that future AI systems develop stronger autonomous-awareness-like properties.
- `Peras` - Computability Boundary Atlas
  Track focused on computability limits, complexity boundaries, and the non-substitutable value that remains distinctly human under digital and AI economics.
- `Stoicheia` - Six-Factor AI Economy Map
  Track focused on the six-factor digital/AI macro system: data, storage, compute, algorithms, energy, and AI talent.

## Execution Logic

- plan in the control tower
- research in linked briefs
- build in project repos
- review through audit gates

No project should progress on hype alone.

## Priority Interpretation

- `Agora` stays the first shipping product track.
- `Physis` and `Titan` remain the next execution tracks.
- `Noesis`, `Peras`, and `Stoicheia` are now official long-horizon tracks, but they should begin with narrow, defensible MVP wedges rather than trying to solve their full intellectual scope immediately.
