# Stoicheia - Six-Factor AI Economy Map

## Status

Archived as a historical planning document.

This line has been folded into `Noesis` as the AI-economy substrate program and
is now publicly carried under the `Polis` umbrella.

## Position

Sixth priority, macro-systems incubation.

## Product Thesis

Build an open framework that models the AI-era world economy through six core factors:

- data
- storage
- compute
- algorithms
- energy
- AI talent

The goal is to make the strategic structure of the AI economy legible enough to support decision-making, forecasting, and systems analysis.

## First Milestones

- formalize the six-factor model and its boundary conditions
- define how the factors interact across digital industry, AI industry, and macroeconomic development
- identify the first usable interface for strategy, policy, or ecosystem mapping
- scaffold the repo for maps, models, and system narratives
