# Peras - Computability Boundary Atlas

## Position

Fifth priority, theory-to-product incubation.

## Product Thesis

Build an open framework that maps the computational limits of digital and AI systems and translates those limits into a clearer theory of durable human value and non-substitutable human capability.

## First Milestones

- define the core thesis boundary around computability and human irreducibility
- map the relevant links between computability theory, complexity theory, digital economics, and AI economics
- identify the first practical surface for turning these ideas into usable decision support
- scaffold the repo for theorem notes, argument maps, and product framing
